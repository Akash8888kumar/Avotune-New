Section 05

Refund Policy

We want every student to feel confident in their decision to enroll. Our refund policy is as follows:

  • Cancellation before batch start (7+ days): 100% refund of fees paid, processed within 10 working days.
  • Cancellation before batch start (less than 7 days): 75% refund. The 25% retention covers administrative and reservation costs.
  • Cancellation within first 2 weeks of batch: 50% refund, subject to written request and approval.
  • Cancellation after 2 weeks of batch: No refund. Students may request a batch transfer (see below).
  • Medical/emergency cancellation: Assessed on a case-by-case basis with supporting documentation. A partial or full refund or free batch transfer may be offered.

Batch Transfer: Students who cannot continue may transfer to a future batch (within 12 months) for a one-time transfer fee of ₹2,000, subject to availability. Batch transfers are not available after 50% of the program has been completed.

To request a refund or batch transfer, email hello@avotunesudio.in with your enrollment details and reason.
